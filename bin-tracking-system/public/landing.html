About EcoTrack

EcoTrack is a smart waste management interface designed for administrators and field teams. With real-time insights, alert-driven prioritization, and location-based tracking, EcoTrack helps cities reduce overflow events, streamline collection schedules, and improve cleanliness outcomes.
